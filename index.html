Frequently Asked Questions

How does the expense tracker work?

Add transactions with amount, category, date, and optional description. Set a monthly budget to track spending progress. View breakdowns by category or month. All calculations happen instantly in your browser.

Is my data stored online?

No. All data stays in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your financial information never leaves your device. Clear browser data = clear app data.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. Interface adapts to phone, tablet, and desktop screens. Works in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). No app download needed.

How do I delete transactions?

Click the "Delete" button on any transaction card. Confirm deletion when prompted. Budget and category totals update automatically.

How does dark mode work?

Click the moon/sun icon in the navigation bar to toggle between dark and light themes. Your preference is saved automatically and persists across sessions.

Do I need an account?

No account required. Just open the page and start tracking. Since data stays local, you don't need to sign up, log in, or remember any passwords.
